超时髦电影La Matriarca~

I am so inspired by Italian movie La Matriarca! Cant believe it was released in 70’s!

The movie itself tells a story of liberation of woman by charactering Mimi, the heroine, discovering her own sexuality by seducing various men and finally found her Mr. right, a charming professor, who’s as well as a doctor and behaves like a pragmatic philosopher. While the part I’m more interested in is the way this film present itself which is absolutely fashionable and stylish, especially Mimi’s wearing!!!

Mimi参加丈夫葬礼时穿的这套， so gorgeous! Black and white,领子和帽檐的花纹，领口的蝴蝶结，都充满了纯情少女的气息，well, 当然还有up up的fashion sense。话说其实从服装上就可以看出这时mimi内心依然十分childish ~




电影中mimi的丈夫偷偷拥有一间时髦到飞起来的apartment，她丈夫去世后,mimi第一次来到这间公寓时的穿着。I adore her cloak and mary jane shoes~
Oh, and her black handbag as well. 第三张图里，有没有觉得卧室祖母绿和香槟金的设计风格美翻了。。



Mimi’s party costume~这个发型和裙子match得很好有没有。



One of Mimi’s acquaintance. Can u feel her femme fatale? I can……Oh, btw, the necklace she’s wearing is an alive beetle on the back of which embedded with colorful gems, can u even believe it?! 电影里有给甲虫项链很多特写，特特特特美！虽然有点残忍，但是甲虫项链这个点子太绝妙了有没有。。And it even reminded me great Allen Poe’s The Golden-Bug and Franz Kafka’s The Metamorphosis. It seems like the beetle was an insect born out with some special mysterious and psychedelic temperament, man, I am totally obsessed!


Is that a pair of roger vivier on her feet?




这套。。事后装。。也很美！It looks like a lovely pinky little fox wrapped around her neck. And, 第二张图中，mimi正走向的是她的wardrobe，且看：


It’s like a dream有没有？！The ultimate dream of every woman is such a magnificent giant wardrobe!




正在看书的女主角迷死人。这三张图片里的沙发也好想要，特别是第二张的透明柠黄充气沙发，电影里卧室里面还有一张一模一样，但颜色全透明的。





故意打扮成女学生去上课。这学院风走得炉火纯青有没有。To draw the professor’s attention, she studied hard and successfully pointed out a little mistake of professor when having class so that he asked her to be his assistance, how smart!






Dating dating dating…



In these two pictures, they just married eath other. And the story ends.事实上女主角一开始只是想要一段affir无奈教授人格魅力无法阻挡（有点玛丽苏），里面各种曲折我就不细说了。。anyway, 电影是个happy ending. 教授的家装好好看，很童趣。

Here are my two favorite lines :

“Various literally sources describe Phyllis astride Aristotle. She refused him the gift of her virginity. She accepts, however, to fulfill a request: she rode Aristotle in the garden, and found in it a particular pleasure, because she was dominating the man.”

“Yes. Freud knew very well how to explain certain things…Modern civilization is based on suppression, on the repression of natural instincts. This makes man(human) unhappy…Sexuality is a serious, important thing, and must above all make you happy…It takes imagination. And absolute sincerity between partners. We must hide nothing of each other. Absolutely nohing. And we must learn to love life, considering it a game, a game for two, where all is possible when we’re love, even on horse back. But I beg you, Mimi, try not to be too fat…This is beautiful, love astride.”

I adore you, my little horse~
