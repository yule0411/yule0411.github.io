Jimi Hendrix in London

Last Friday, I came by and paied a visit to Jimi Hendrix’s apartment in Brook street 25. Just then, it occurs to me that I better write down my experience for a better memory. There I go.


Just as expected, his bedroom was very Jimi style and a woman there told me this place is currently used for shooting, too.
Jimi was born to be a passionate artist who had a childlike temperament, just listen to his musics and you’ll know, and it seems that all realistic matters were not in his considerations.
He lived by his own, so beautiful, so warm.










I took some photos in the record room which are all about the record collections owned by Jimi. I might spend some spare time to sort out and make a song list to better share with my friends. Gosh, we are so lucky to have Jimi’s record collections.
